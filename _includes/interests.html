About Me

I am a graduate student studying Romance Linguistics in the Romance Languages and Literatures Ph.D. program at the University of California, Berkeley. My research interests include bilingual phonetics and phonology, language contact, and experimental and quantitative methods. I focus primarily on Spanish and Catalan in the U.S., as well as in Catalonia, Spain.
